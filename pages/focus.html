Enhanced Focus

Eastman Kodak | Lead Design, Research Planning & Co-Facilitation | 2010

As lead designer under the design director with minimal guidance, I drove the redesign of the Kodak Picture Maker Kiosk to counter growing competition in the photo-kiosk space. The goal was to modernize its visual appeal and boost perceived quality by centering the experience on users‘ own images.

New Design

The redesign minimized visual clutter, spotlighting user-generated content to create a more engaging and premium feel for kiosk users.
