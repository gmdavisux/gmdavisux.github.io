Increasing Success

Coupa makes it possible for purchasers and suppliers to connect and do business. We had a problem that suppliers often failed to complete their profile and so were unable to conduct business with purchasers.

User Research

The onboarding process involved providing info regarding business, financial, shipping, banking, identity, diversity and more. Our research found that all but the smallest businesses required information from several people or departments in order to complete the onboarding. Once left incomplete, the missing information was sometimes never completed.

Workflow

We needed to ask multiple questions for onboarding.

  • My first instinct was to simplify by removing questions. Unfortunately, all of the questions were necessary to do business.
  • We couldn't allow any one question to block the rest, so allowing the user to skip questions was essential.
  • I provided a method for users to skip steps.
  • Based on our research, I made it easy to forward unanswered questions directly to a colleague.

Details

Choosing a "Skip" link below the Next button would show a menu to skip or forward to a colleague. Forwarding would open a message in your default email client — so the message would come from you, not the app.

Forward using your own email client

I devised sample code for a standard mailto link as a proof of concept for the team. Try it, it works!

Forward to a colleague

Onboarding success improved by 50% over previous figures which played an instrumental role in enabling $43 billion in value for diverse suppliers.
