Filtering Profiles

IT administrators need a way to automate finding and updating machines on their network. Filtering the machines by location, conditions (such as idleness, time of day or operating system) is important. Actions to be performed may include mapping a drive, installign a print-driver or installing software updates.

Upon first visiting an existing profile, the accordion sections are collapsed.

Conditions (Validation Logic)

The conditions section may contain one or more inherited profiles. more can be added. Hovering over profile boxes will reveal additional control to delete or reorder a linked profile.
